It wasn't love at first sight!

It was not love at first sight! Just like it wasn't in his eyes, it wasn't in his voice or how he walks. It was friendship that had been through storms of various other relationships with no glint of hope of ever loving him. It wasn't magic or poetry. It was a young man that constantly assured me of how much he's loved me from a dinner party some four years ago to the current breathe of fresh air that I breathe.

It was a constant battle of me fighting my emotions to make sure I wasn't in love with a man that was just too good to be true. It was the urge to seek someone else. Someone who wouldn't let me win arguments for the love of good conversations. Or so I thought .

It was basically me stressing about my whole life looking for the perfect one for me even when I had him in front of me all along. It was a journey of hurt, broken ego, unrequited love, undeserved relationship that led me to the truth that is named "Abdulgafar ".

I love you!

And just as you might have guessed, it was me realizing how much I'd be missing an actual gold of a man if I kept on chasing stones. It was a realisation that he has always and will always be there for me that made me figure; this is it. It wasn't love. It wasn't love until I became his wife and I couldn't bare to see him hurt, hungry, cold or whatever stupid thing that I didn't use to care about.

Why? Because I could never really explain what I felt for him. I didn't know if it was love or not. But it was packed with care, tolerance, understanding, patience, trust that I was ready to give to him. And with that huge part, I understood that maybe I was just scared to use the word "love" because it sounded more like a responsibility than a feeling.

But right now, responsibility, feeling? I don't really care because I probably would never sacrifice for anyone else what I'll sacrifice for him. And as each day passes, I understand that what I feel is actually called LOVE.

My baby, I love you more than my eyes could express and my tongue could ever say. But when you catch me staring, it's a glimpse of how this hard guy has fallen so hard for you. You're a good man; maybe the best and you totally deserve an adorable human like me. *scoffs I pray to be with you in Jannah ❤. I LOVE YOU!
